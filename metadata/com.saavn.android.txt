Saavn brings you free, unlimited access to all your favorite Bollywood, Indian regional, and even English* music – wherever you go.

Find your favorite songs and artists, create your own playlists, enjoy sets from our expert curators, or play radio from any song or artist - Saavn delivers the perfect music, every time.

From the newest songs to hard-to-find classics, Saavn’s catalog includes millions of tracks across Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Punjabi, Marathi, Bengali, Kannada, Gujarati, Malayalam, and Bhojpuri. Listen to all the best film music, bhangra, bhajans, ghazals, pop, remixes, and more.
