Summary:AntennaPod
Description:
AntennaPod is a podcast manager for Android 2.3.3 and above. It offers all the basic features you expect from a podcatcher, like streaming and downloading episodes, refreshing all feeds automatically or adding them to a queue to listen to them later. The Miro directory can be searched and browsed for shows.
