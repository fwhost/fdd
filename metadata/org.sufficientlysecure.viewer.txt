Description:
Document Viewer is a fork of the last GPL version of EBookDroid. Supports:

    PDF
    DjVu
    XPS (OpenXPS)
    Comic Books (cbz) (NO support for cbr (rar compressed))
    FictionBook (fb2, fb2.zip)

This apk supports all ABIs: ARM, x86 and MIPS.
