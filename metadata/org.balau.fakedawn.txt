Summary:Fake Dawn
Description:
Fake Dawn is an alarm clock that gradually increases brightness and sound volume to lead you out of deep sleep and wake you up gently. You can adjust when the brightness of the screen starts to rise and reaches its maximum; when the sound starts to play and how the volume increases.

Features:

    fully customize the behavior of the dawn alarm
    choose the screen color from a wide range
    choose custom sound alarm or ring tone
    vibrate with sound
.
