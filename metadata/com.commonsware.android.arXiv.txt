Keep up with the latest science research: physics, math, computers science. Browse daily science articles (Physics, Astronomy, Math...) at arXiv.org and search the entire arXiv collection. A homescreen widget lets you know of any new articles in your favorite science categories (dozens of physics, math, computer science categories) or custom searches.
