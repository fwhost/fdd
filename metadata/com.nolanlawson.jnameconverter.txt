Summary:Japanese Name Converter
Description:
Converts any name from English into Japanese characters. Learn to write and pronounce your name in katakana, or even create a good approximation in kanji! Works best on common American first names. Combine different kanji to change the meaning, save your favorites, share via Identi.ca, email, SMS, etc.
