Summary:India Satellite Weather
Description:
"A picture is worth a thousand words". A complex weather data can be conveyed with a single image map. By observing the cloud formations around your City, you can predict whether the day is going to be Hot or Rainy/Cloudy.

The app fetches the weather data directly from the Official Indian Government webpage for "satellite Images & Products"

A couple of proprietary libraries were removed.
